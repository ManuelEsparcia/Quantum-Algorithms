Loading a Quantum Dot

Everything here is a voltage — the same control a transistor uses. Form the well, load exactly one electron, then rotate its spin.

The device

Raise the gate voltage to deepen the well until it holds exactly one electron.

Controls

Gate voltage empty0.30 V
Deepens the potential well. Too shallow = no electron; too deep = two.
Spin drive (EDSR)off
Rotates the electron spin. Only works once one electron is loaded.
Spin readout appears once a single electron is loaded.
